About us

We are the Weather Station Project Dev team. We are a team of four high school students and a college student from all around the world right from Oregon, USA to Pasig, Philippines who all have had our fair share of hurdles when we were starting out in the world of electronics and the Internet of Things.

We came together under the thought of creating a unified and structured way to a project. We, and many others’ dream is to have ample amounts of resources for a project, especially when starting out. We believe that learning happens only when we hit an obstacle, an error, but the need for resources to aid the rectification of the error is also a crucial part in learning.

Why this project?

One of the most famous first projects, the ‘Hello World’ of the Internet of Things, is obtaining sensor data and sending it to a database for collection. This type of project not only covers most of the basics of IoT, it also acts as a gateway to other, more complex projects down the line.

Although this may seem like the perfect project for beginners, the need to learn several programming languages like Javascript, PHP, C++/MicroPython, SQL for creating the Database, code the microcontroller and make a website may be a daunting task for beginners. This is the reason we chose to create a simple home Weather Station where the user only needs to care about the Microcontroller and use our premade API initially and can choose to learn other aspects of the project at their own pace.
